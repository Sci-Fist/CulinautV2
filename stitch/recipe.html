24h Fermentation Sourdough

A masterclass in hydration control and enzymatic activity. This recipe follows the clinical Culinaut standard for high-volume bakery operations.

Target pH 3.95
Core Temp 96°C
Salinity 2.2%

Ingredients & Tools

  • 700g Strong Bread Flour
  • 525g Water (Filtered, 24°C)
  • 150g Active Levain (100% hydration)
  • 15g Sea Salt (Maldon)
01. Autolyse

Mix flour and water until no dry spots remain. Let rest for 60 minutes at 22°C to allow initial gluten development and starch conversion.

02. Inoculation

Incorporate levain and salt using the Rubaud method. Maintain dough temperature at 24°C. Check pH baseline.

03. Bulk Fermentation

Perform four sets of coil folds every 30 minutes. Monitor for 20% volume increase and a pH drop of 0.5 units.
